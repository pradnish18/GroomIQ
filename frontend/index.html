FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GroomIQ

How does face shape detection work?▼
GroomIQ uses OpenCV-based facial analysis to detect your face shape from uploaded photos. It analyzes facial proportions including jaw width, face height, and cheekbone structure to classify your face into one of 7 shapes: Oval, Round, Square, Rectangle, Diamond, Heart, or Triangle.
Is my data secure?▼
Yes. All images are encrypted, passwords are hashed using bcrypt, and we use JWT-based authentication. Your data is never shared with third parties.
How accurate is the analysis?▼
Our face shape detection achieves 85%+ accuracy with well-lit, front-facing photos. For best results, use natural lighting and avoid extreme angles.
Can I cancel my premium subscription?▼
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your premium features remain active until the end of your billing period.
What file formats are supported?▼
We support JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats with a maximum file size of 10MB.
